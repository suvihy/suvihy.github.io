Educational structure of population
in Finland, years 1998, 2008 and 2018

By the end of 2018, altogether 3,399,532 persons, or 73 per cent of the population aged 15 or over had completed a post-comprehensive level qualification. Seventy-six per cent of the population with Finnish background had completed post-comprehensive level qualifications, while the corresponding share for persons with foreign background was 51 per cent.

Source: Findicator. Statistics Finland 2019.
